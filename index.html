Setup:

The game is designed for 2 to 4 players, from 12 years old and more

A game lasts about 20 minutes

Shuffle the "property" cards, deal them 1 by 1 (10 for each player), those cards are set inline in front of the players

Shuffle the "value" cards and special cards, deal them 1 by 1 (5 for each player)

Each player should always have 5 cards in their hand

The youngest player starts, then play proceeds clockwise.

Rules:

Each turn, the player has 3 options:

  • Place an appropriate value on their own property cards
  • Place an inappropriate value on their opponents' property cards to create a 'bug'
  • Fix a bug on one of their own property cards by discarding the bad value and replacing it with good value

If a player plays a "senior dev" card, he/she can fix 1 to 3 bugs on his/her board at the same time by putting a "fix" token on the inapropriate values on their board

If a player plays a "junior dev" card, he/she can put 1 to 3 cards in his/her hand on 1, 2 or 3 opponents to put "bugs" on their board

First player to complete his/her stylesheet without any bug wins the game

Content:

40 x property cards

100 x value cards

15 x "fix" tokens

special cards :

5 senior dev cards

5 junior dev cards
